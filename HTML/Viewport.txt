Viewport => ClientWidth and ClientHeight
  - they don't include scrollbars; use innerWidth and innerHeight;

Browser window size => window.outerWidth and window.outerHeight


Screen resolution:
  - real resolution => window.screen.height and window.inner.width
  - available resolution => window.screen.availHeight and window.screen.availWidth


Document size
  - document.body.clientWidth and document.body.clientHeight
